Privacy Policy for Firma Tespit Eklentisi

Last Updated: March 25, 2025

Firma Tespit Eklentisi ("the Extension") is designed to detect and notify users about websites affiliated with specific companies. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

The Extension collects anonymous usage data via Google Analytics to improve functionality and user experience. This data includes:

  • Interactions with the Extension, such as button clicks (e.g., "Update List" or "Show List") and popup openings.
  • No personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email, or IP address, is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used solely for:

  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance the Extension’s performance and features.
  • No data is shared with third parties except Google Analytics, which processes it anonymously.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics, a third-party service, to collect and analyze usage data. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies. For more information, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Data Storage and Security

Usage data is stored securely by Google Analytics and is not linked to individual users. We do not store any personal data locally or on external servers beyond the anonymous analytics data.

5. Your Choices

You can disable the Extension at any time through your browser settings if you do not wish to share usage data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at [info@burakkp.com].
